## Processing

Orders are typically processed within **1–2 business days** after payment and verification.
Orders may be delayed or canceled if payment verification fails, fraud is suspected, or for
inventory or regulatory reasons.

## Shipping methods & timeframes

- Orders ship via a tracked carrier service.
- Estimated transit time is typically **2–5 business days** within the domestic shipping region
  after dispatch. Transit times are estimates and not guarantees.
- Shipping options and costs are shown at checkout.

## Handling

Products are supplied as lyophilized powder and packaged for safe transit. Handle and store per
the laboratory reference information provided and your institution's safety guidelines.

## Tracking

A tracking number is emailed when your order ships. You can follow it on the **Track Order**
page. If you have not received tracking, contact us.

## Shipping regions

We ship to eligible destinations only. Some products or destinations may be restricted by
applicable law; we reserve the right to refuse or cancel orders that cannot be shipped
compliantly.

## Undeliverable / refused shipments

If a shipment is returned as undeliverable or refused, we will contact you to arrange
re-shipment (additional shipping charges may apply) or a refund of the product value, less
original shipping.
