## All sales final

Due to the nature of research chemicals and to preserve chain-of-custody and product integrity,
**all sales are final** and products are **non-returnable**, except where a refund or replacement
is required by applicable law or expressly stated below.

## Damaged, defective, or incorrect orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, contact us within **7 days** of delivery
at **FuturisticScienceUSA@gmail.com** with your order number and clear photos of the item and
packaging. If we confirm the issue, we will provide a **replacement or refund** of the affected
item(s).

Please do not discard the product or packaging until the claim is resolved.

## Non-refundable situations

We cannot accept returns or issue refunds for:

- Products that have been opened, used, or altered
- Change-of-mind requests after an order has shipped
- Orders refused or returned as undeliverable (see Shipping Policy)
- Issues reported after the claim window

## Order verification & cancellations

We may delay or cancel any order if payment verification fails, fraud is suspected, or for
inventory or regulatory reasons. Verified cancellations are refunded to the original payment
method.

## Refund processing

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Please allow the payment provider's
standard processing time for the funds to appear.
