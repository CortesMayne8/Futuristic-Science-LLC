# About Futuristic Science

**Peptides you can trust. Purity you can prove.**

Futuristic Science, LLC supplies research-grade peptides to qualified researchers and
institutions. We exist for one reason: to make research materials whose identity and purity
can be independently verified — every batch, every time.

## Trust through proof

We don't ask you to take our word for it. Every product we ship is:

- **Third-party tested** by independent laboratories for identity and purity
- **Backed by a batch-specific Certificate of Analysis (COA)** you can look up by batch number
- **Labeled with measured mg content**, not nominal or rounded figures
- **99%+ purity**, verified by HPLC

## What we do — and don't — do

Futuristic Science is a research supply company. Our products are sold **strictly for
laboratory research use only**. They are **not** approved for human or animal consumption,
and are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. We do not provide
dosing guidance, usage instructions, or any advice regarding application. Handling and storage
information is provided only as laboratory reference for qualified researchers.

## How we work

- **Independent verification.** We partner with third-party labs so purity and identity are
  confirmed by someone other than us.
- **Traceability.** Every vial ties back to a batch and a published COA.
- **Careful handling.** Products are supplied as lyophilized powder and shipped with lab-handling
  reference information.
- **Straight answers.** Our team can help with product specifications, documentation,
  availability, ordering, and shipping — we cannot advise on research protocols or applications.
