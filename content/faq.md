# Frequently Asked Questions

> For Research Purposes Only. Not for human use. The answers below cover products,

## Products & quality

**Are your products tested?**
Yes. Every batch is tested by an independent third-party laboratory for identity and purity,
and ships with a batch-specific Certificate of Analysis (COA).

**What purity can I expect?**
Our products are verified at 99%+ purity by HPLC. The exact measured value for a given batch is
listed on that batch's COA.

**How do I find a COA?**
Use the **COA Library** and search by your batch number, or find the "View Certificate of
Analysis" link on the product page.

**What does "measured accuracy" mean?**
Each vial is labeled with the measured mg content rather than a nominal figure, so the amount on
the label reflects what the batch actually contains.

**What form do products ship in?**
As lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder, intended for in-vitro laboratory research by qualified
researchers.

## Handling & storage (lab reference only)

**How should I store the product?**
General laboratory reference: store lyophilized powder cold as indicated on the product
documentation, and handle in accordance with your institution's safety guidelines. This is
handling information only and is not usage guidance.
