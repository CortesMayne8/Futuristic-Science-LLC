Compact status / attribute label — purity %, "Verified", stock state, "Featured".

```jsx
<Badge tone="teal" variant="soft">COA Verified</Badge>
<Badge tone="gold" variant="solid">99%+ Purity</Badge>
<Badge tone="success">In Stock</Badge>
```

- **tone:** `teal` (verified/COA), `gold` (featured/purity), `neutral`, `success`/`warning`/`danger` (stock & status), `ink`.
- **variant:** `soft` (tinted bg, default), `solid` (filled), `outline` (hairline).
- Always pill-shaped; pass `iconLeft` for a small Lucide glyph.
