The mandatory **"For Research Purposes Only. Not for human use."** notice. Required on hero, product pages, footer, cart, checkout, and FAQ.

```jsx
<DisclaimerBanner variant="strip" />
<DisclaimerBanner variant="ink" detail="No medical or human-use claims. Reconstitution = lab handling only." />
<DisclaimerBanner variant="inline" />
```

- **strip:** thin ink top-bar for site-wide placement.
- **ink:** prominent navy block with gold rule — hero/footer.
- **inline:** soft gold note for product detail.
- Shield-check icon is built in. Never alter the core text.
